PyroSense

Advanced fire monitoring and prediction system for enhanced safety and rapid response.

Prediction Phase

  • Advanced algorithms analyze weather, vegetation, and temperature data.
  • High-risk zones are identified based on historical and real-time inputs.
  • Drones are prioritized based on target severity and stay on watch in high-priority areas.

Detection Phase

  • Drones are deployed based on predictions.
  • They navigate to high-risk zones automatically.
  • Sensors confirm fire presence in real-time.
  • Feedback is sent back to validate predictions.

Propagation Phase

  • Estimates fire spread rate based on wind, temperature, and vegetation.
  • Predicts affected areas based on fire direction and propagation patterns.
  • Calculates duration using intensity and weather conditions.
